01 Getting Started
📱 Launching the App
  1. Tap the Launch App button above or go to zitofiber.vercel.app/?team=fiber — no download required, opens directly in your browser.
  2. Add to Home Screen for one-tap access: tap Share → "Add to Home Screen" in Safari or Chrome. Works offline once installed.
  3. On the Setup Screen, enter your full name (First Last — must match the Reps sheet exactly), then tap Load My Territory.
  4. Your address list, territory assignment, and rep profile (phone/email) load automatically from the team Google Sheet.
  5. Tap Launch FieldOS to enter the field view.
💡
Offline Mode: Once loaded, the app works without a signal. Addresses, map tiles, and previously visited data are cached locally. Submissions sync automatically when you reconnect.

💼 When You Make a Sale
  1. Tap the address pin on the map or find it in the sidebar → tap to open the Sales Form.
  2. Select the Mega Speed or Gig Speed package card. Pricing details appear automatically.
  3. Fill in the customer's First Name, Last Name, and Phone (required). Email and notes are optional.
  4. Use the Schedule Picker to book the install — choose from available Mon–Fri slots (8AM, 10AM, 1PM, 3PM).
  5. The proration breakdown auto-calculates when you select the install date. Walk the customer through it on the spot.
  6. Tap Submit. The app logs the sale, updates the map pin, and syncs to the team sheet instantly.

06 Tips & Best Practices
🔍 Use the Search Bar

Type a street name to instantly filter the address list — great for jumping to a specific block.

📝 Always Add Notes

Note a dog, best time to return, or any context in the notes field. It syncs to the sheet and helps on callbacks.

🔄 Refresh Your Data

Tap the refresh button (⟳) any time to pull the latest addresses and teammate updates from the sheet.

🗓️ Book Installs on the Spot

Live slots show in the Schedule Picker. Book with the customer at the door to maximize your close rate.

⏱️ Work Follow-ups Daily

Switch to "Follow-ups" each morning. Soft-interest leads age fast — revisit within 24–48 hours.

📶 Low Signal Areas

Submissions queue locally when offline and sync automatically when you're back in range — nothing is lost.
