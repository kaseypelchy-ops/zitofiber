01 Getting Started
📱 Launching the App
  1. Scan the QR code above (or go to zitofiber.vercel.app/?team=sfi) — your browser will open the app. No download needed.
  2. Add to Home Screen for fastest access: tap the share icon in Safari/Chrome → "Add to Home Screen." The app installs as a PWA — works offline once loaded.
  3. On the Setup Screen, enter your full name (First Last — must match the Reps sheet exactly) and tap Load My Territory.
  4. Your address list and territory will load automatically from the team Google Sheet. Your phone and email profile will also populate.
  5. Tap Launch FieldOS to enter the field view.
💡
Offline mode: Once launched, the app works without a signal. Your address list, map tiles, and all previously visited addresses are cached locally. Submissions sync as soon as you reconnect.
02 The Map & Address List
🛰️
Satellite View
Real aerial imagery with street labels overlaid. You can see house driveways and identify residences vs. businesses before you knock.

Color-Coded Pins
Every address pin is colored by status — gray (pending), purple/green (sold), amber (not home), red (fiber competitor), pink (not interested), cyan (go back), and more.
⚡
Existing Customers
Yellow bolt ⚡ icons are current Zito subscribers. Do NOT attempt to sell to these addresses — they're already on service.
🗺️
Coverage Heat Map
Tap "Coverage Map" to see a color-filled overlay of your progress. Helps spot uncovered clusters at a glance.
🧭
Route Mode
Tap "Route Mode" to re-sort your address list by distance from your current GPS location — nearest homes first for efficient routing.
⏱️
Stale Follow-ups
Tap "Follow-ups" to see addresses marked "Go Back," "Maybe Later," or "Not Home" that are due for a revisit, sorted by how long ago you knocked.
📌
Pin Drop: If you find a home not in your list, tap the map to drop a custom pin, reverse-geocode the address, and add it as a new lead — it gets written back to the shared sheet automatically.

💼 When You Make a Sale
  1. Tap the address pin or find it in the sidebar list → tap to open the Sales Form.
  2. Select Mega Speed or Gig Speed package card. Pricing details appear automatically.
  3. Fill in customer's First Name, Last Name, and Phone (required). Email and notes are optional but helpful.
  4. Use the Schedule Picker to book the install appointment — pick a date and available time slot (Mon–Fri, 8AM/10AM/1PM/3PM).
  5. The proration breakdown auto-calculates when you pick the install date. Show this to the customer — they appreciate knowing exactly what they'll pay.
  6. Tap Submit — Mega Speed or Submit — Gig Speed. The app logs the sale, turns the map pin purple/green, and syncs to the team sheet.

06 Tips & Best Practices
🔍 Use the Search Bar

Type a street name to instantly filter the address list. Great for jumping to a specific block without scrolling.

📝 Add Notes

The notes field on every disposition syncs to the team sheet. Note a dog, best time to return, or any customer context — it helps on callbacks.

🔄 Refresh Your Data

Tap the refresh button (⟳) at any time to pull the latest addresses and teammate updates from the shared sheet.

🗓️ Book Installs on the Spot

Live install slots are shown in the Schedule Picker. Available slots are marked green — book one with the customer standing at the door to maximize close rate.

⏱️ Work Your Follow-ups Daily

Switch to "Follow-ups" mode each morning. Soft-interest addresses (Go Back, Maybe Later, Talk to Spouse) age quickly — revisit within 24–48 hours.

📶 Low Signal Areas

The app queues submissions locally when offline. Once you're back in range, everything syncs automatically — no data is lost.
